General information

Factory Invasion - a single modification for the Half-life computer game, developed since September 2019 by Ukrainian programmer and game designer Mikhail Samarin (aka The Sam), it tells the story of Daniel Gibs, an ordinary worker at the hazardous waste center in New Mexico, who by the will of circumstances, he could survive after a cascade resonance, and now there is only one task - to get out of the industrial complex teeming with zen creatures.

Developing timeline

September 22, 2019 - Creation of the official community modification VKontakte, and the official start of development

September 25, 2019 - A modification page was created on ModDB

October 15, 2019 - System Requirements Announced

October 22, 2019 - Release of the first demo version of the modification

October 31, 2019 - Hotfix 1.1 for the demo version of the update released

November 17, 2019 - List of soundtracks announced

December 9, 2019 - Start of transferring existing developments to Trinity Render

December 21, 2019 - Release of the toolkit for developers version 1.0

 

Technologies

At the moment, the modification is being ported to the Trinity Rendering Engine, since it allows:

 

- Create three-dimensional detailed explosions

- Draw shader water, reflections

- Draw soft shadows from creatures and the world

- Support for lighting effects like on Valve`s Source Engine
